# Rootis — Lore & Flavor

> Narrative and flavor for Rootis. **Mechanics live in [`pcs/rootis.yaml`](../pcs/rootis.yaml)** —
> Rootis is a stock vanilla FE8 class with no per-character abilities. Everything below is
> story/flavor only: it does **not** drive gameplay. The ability "fantasies" are preserved here
> (migrated from pre-strip commit `e6cc7a6`) as inspiration for dialogue, sprite/palette work,
> and signature scripted moments — never as FE mechanics.

## Concept

- **Race:** Snowperson
- **D&D class:** Sorcerer (Draconic Bloodline)
- **Dragon ancestor:** White Dragon
- **Alignment:** neutral_good
- **Size/age:** medium

## Personality

- **Personality:** There's nothing I like more than a good mystery.
- **Ideal:** No Limits. Nothing should fetter the infinite possibility inherent in all existence. (Chaotic)
- **Flaw:** I overlook obvious solutions in favor of complicated ones.

Frostquake — the packed snow underfoot bucks and splinters into shards.
