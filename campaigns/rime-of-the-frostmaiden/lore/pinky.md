# Pinky — Lore & Flavor

> Narrative and flavor for Pinky. **Mechanics live in [`npcs/pinky.yaml`](../npcs/pinky.yaml)** —
> Pinky is a stock vanilla FE8 Pegasus Knight (the army's flier), not an RBG summon. Everything
> below is story/flavor only.

## Concept

Pinky is Prof. R.B. Geenius's homunculus "son" — a constructed being RBG made and raises as
family. Pinky fills the army's **flier role**: a Pegasus Knight (lance flier, MOV 7) who promotes
to Falcon Knight. The "homunculus" nature is pure flavor; in-game Pinky is an ordinary mounted
flier with vanilla stats.

- **Built by / parent:** Prof. R.B. Geenius
- **Role:** flier (Pegasus Knight → Falcon Knight)
- **Recruited:** available from Chapter 1

## The Wish (finale beat)

The campaign's emotional payoff: at the finale, RBG spends one of the two Wish charges from his
**Luck E. Cheese** blade to turn Pinky from a homunculus into a true living being — making his
"son" real. This is a scripted story event, not a gameplay mechanic. (See the `story_only` Wish
item in [`pcs/prof-rbg.yaml`](../pcs/prof-rbg.yaml).)
