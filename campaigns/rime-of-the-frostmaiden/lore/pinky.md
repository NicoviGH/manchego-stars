# Pinky — Lore & Flavor

> Narrative and flavor for Pinky. **Mechanics live in [`pcs/pinky.yaml`](../pcs/pinky.yaml)** —
> Pinky is the 8th PC and a lord candidate: a stock vanilla FE8 Pegasus Knight (the army's flier),
> not an RBG summon. Everything below is story/flavor only.

## Concept

Pinky is Prof. R.B. Geenius's homunculus "son" — a constructed being RBG made and raises as
family. Pinky fills the army's **flier role**: a Pegasus Knight (lance flier, MOV 7) who promotes
to Falcon Knight. The "homunculus" nature is pure flavor; in-game Pinky is an ordinary mounted
flier with vanilla stats.

- **Built by / parent:** Prof. R.B. Geenius
- **Role:** flier (Pegasus Knight → Falcon Knight)
- **Joins:** founding party — deployed at the Northlook in Ch1 with the rest of the cast (8th PC, lord candidate)
- **Pronouns:** he/him

## Voice

Childlike and utterly reverential toward his father: Pinky exists to please RBG, and
he'll put himself in danger to do it (he volunteered as the scout in the mine). At
the table he and RBG were played by the same person — Pinky was mechanically one of
RBG's constructs — so their dynamic is a single performer's double act: the proud,
grandiose father and the earnest little son. (Interview with Nicolas, 2026-06-10.)

**Diction rules**
- Childlike, simple, sincere sentences; bright and eager. Earnest, not stupid.
- Reverential to RBG: addresses him as "Father" (never "RBG" or "Professor"), takes
  his orders as gospel, and lights up at any scrap of praise.
- Eager to please to a fault: volunteers for dangerous jobs before anyone asks — the
  party's worried "Pinky, no" is the recurring beat; Pinky's "Father said I could!"
  is the reply.
- Innocent read on the world: he takes things literally and assumes the best,
  including about his father (he doesn't see the lawful-evil part).
- The homunculus nature is matter-of-fact to him, never angsty — saving the "am I
  real?" weight for the finale Wish; don't spend it early.

**Calibration moment (table-derived, paraphrased)**
- The mine: RBG sends Pinky ahead as the scout; Pinky goes proudly into the danger
  because his father asked. Voice target: "Father needs to know what's down there.
  I'll be right back!"

**Banned:** sarcasm, cynicism, world-weariness, fear of serving, resentment toward
RBG, robot/construct-speak ("DIRECTIVE ACCEPTED"), early existential angst,
disobedience played straight.

## The Wish (finale beat)

The campaign's emotional payoff: at the finale, RBG spends one of the two Wish charges from his
**Luck E. Cheese** blade to turn Pinky from a homunculus into a true living being — making his
"son" real. This is a scripted story event, not a gameplay mechanic. (See the `story_only` Wish
item in [`pcs/prof-rbg.yaml`](../pcs/prof-rbg.yaml).)
