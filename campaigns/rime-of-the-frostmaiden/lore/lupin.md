# Lupin — the bespectacled direwolf (ch04 recruit) — Lore & Flavor

> Narrative and flavor only. **Mechanics live in [`npcs/lupin.yaml`](../npcs/lupin.yaml)** —
> Cavalier under the hood, wolf sprite on top; vanilla FE8 data throughout.

## Concept

- **Who:** leader of the awakened direwolf pack in the White Moose forest; wears
  spectacles; speaks.
- **Canon status:** TABLE canon, not book canon (Nicolas, 2026-07-03) — but he sits
  squarely on the book's *awaken* magic, which is Ravisin's signature (she awakened
  the white moose; our villain thread adds the wolves and Messie). The unexplained
  spectacles are part of the joke — like Marty's spore-cough, never explained.
- **Role:** ch04 parley target (Marty's secondary signature beat) → sled-team NPC →
  deployable beast-cavalier from ch05.

## Voice

**Blunt pack-pragmatist** (locked with Nicolas, 2026-07-03): speaks well but thinks
like a wolf — everything is meat, territory, odds, and pack survival. He bargains
hard, without flourish, and commits completely once the math favors the deal.

**Diction rules**
- Opens with arithmetic: he counts things aloud — heads, odds, mouths, days of food —
  and negotiates from the numbers. ("Six of us. Eight of you...")
- Everything is valued in food and survival; abstractions get restated as meals or
  risks. Flattery and small talk don't exist.
- Blunt, complete sentences; no growl-speak, no doggy tics, no howling theatrics.
- Addresses Marty as "mushroom" — species-as-name is his habit; earned names come
  later, if ever.
- Once a deal is struck it is absolute pack law: feeding the pack is the other
  side's sacred obligation, and he'll say so.
- The one who awakened the pack is the ONLY subject that shortens his sentences —
  dread expressed as terse avoidance, never named, never explained further in MVP
  (Ravisin's reveal belongs to ch05).
- Scene-direction quirk (pick ONE per scene): he adjusts the spectacles with a paw,
  or peers over them. Never explains them.

**Calibration lines (locked ch04 script, 2026-07-03; trimmed in final review — the
Ravisin dread is consolidated into the single ending line)**
- "Six of us. Eight of you — but three smell like prey. Someone's about to have a
  bad afternoon."
- "The town sends a talking mushroom with... berries. Do I look like a BIRD to you,
  mushroom?"
- "Steady food beats proud starving. The pack pulls your sled. Feed them well,
  mushroom."
- "There. Old elf-stones — and the bone-trees just past. The trail goes in.
  ...Everything she wakes comes home to her, eventually."

**Banned:** pet/doggy behavior (fetching, tail-wagging comedy), servility toward the
party (he's a contractor, not a pet), flowery or courtly speech, naming Ravisin
before ch05, cowardice framing (his avoidance of the bone-trees is pack-preservation
math, not fear he'd admit to), howling/growling rendered as dialogue.
