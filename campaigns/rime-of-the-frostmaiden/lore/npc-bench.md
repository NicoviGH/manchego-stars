# NPC bench — minor/incidental characters (house visits, villagers, shops)

Distilled from **"Icewind Dale: Ten-Towns Hand Drawn Maps and NPC Builder"** (Joel
Kleine, DriveThru 353776 — full tables in
`references/References/NPCs/2410173-Icewind_Dale_NPCs.pdf`; see CREDITS.md).
Use when a scene needs a setting-true villager fast: FE house visits, shop keepers,
one-line NPCs, side recruits. PCs and named story NPCs get real voice bibles instead.

## Four pre-generated wanderers (drop-in, hooks included)

| Name | Who | Voice/quirk | Hook |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Pia** | tiefling girl, escaped the Bryn Shander sacrifice lottery after a councillor framed her family; months alone trapping in the wild | speaks in whispers; despises authority | wants her name cleared — strong FE house-visit or side-recruit material |
| **Laszlo Ofrandus** | elderly half-elf retired miner who fled Waterdeep's bustle, kept moving smaller until Icewind Dale | hoarse; walks with a crutch | paranoid about wildlife; seeks escorts when travelling |
| **Torsten Anvidir** | human carpenter in Good Mead | jovial off-key whistling you hear before you see him; over-enunciates | tracking a long-lost business partner; won't say why ("That's none of your business!") |
| **Jissika Humplebumple** | non-binary gnome scholar, came in with a trade caravan | mumbles to themself | staunch pacifist; trades information about the Dale's changes |
