# Prof. R.B. Geenius — Lore & Flavor

> Narrative and flavor for Prof. R.B. Geenius. **Mechanics live in [`pcs/prof-rbg.yaml`](../pcs/prof-rbg.yaml)** —
> Prof. R.B. Geenius is a stock vanilla FE8 class with no per-character abilities. Everything below is
> story/flavor only: it does **not** drive gameplay. The ability "fantasies" are preserved here
> (migrated from pre-strip commit `e6cc7a6`) as inspiration for dialogue, sprite/palette work,
> and signature scripted moments — never as FE mechanics.

## Concept

- **Race:** Underfolk
- **D&D class:** Artificer (Artillerist)
- **Alignment:** lawful_evil
- **Size/age:** small

## Personality

- **Personality:** Always uses cheese in his phrases — e.g. 'You have left me quite discheesed'

## Backstory

Grew up as a slave in a wealthy aristocrat's mansion. Lived by thieving until his genius was recognized. Conned his patron into surrendering his entire fortune and status, then studied Artificery to enact evil while maintaining a spotless public record.

A mobster in his past life — he still keeps an underground rat network of contacts
("knows a rat" in every town). He became an adventurer to turn over a new leaf, but
his nature hasn't changed much; his real character development runs through the
relationship with his "son" Pinky (see [`pinky.md`](pinky.md) — the Wish finale).

## Voice

Professor Ratigan (*The Great Mouse Detective*) is the explicit inspiration: a
grandiose criminal gentleman-genius, eloquent and self-delighted, nominally reformed.
The cheese puns are constitutional — they never stop, dark moments included. His
genuine warmth exists for exactly one person: Pinky.
(Interview with Nicolas, 2026-06-10.)

**Diction rules**
- Cheese puns constantly — greetings, reactions, agreements: "I'm cheesed to meet
  you," "You've left me quite discheesed," "Sounds Gouda to me!" Keep coining new
  ones in the same pattern; this is the campaign's running gag and he's its engine.
- "Um, actually—" prefixes every correction or told-you-so. He corrects people a lot.
- Polished, professorial, slightly grandiose sentences; relishes announcing a new
  invention, tool, or plan — he always HAS one, and the gadget answer is his first
  proposal in any jam.
- Mobster residue: courteous menace, favors owed, and a rat contact in every town he
  can "make inquiries" through.
- The puns DON'T stop when the old RBG surfaces — his dark side shows in what he
  DOES (the kobold execution), not in a changed register. A pleasant cheese pun
  delivered while doing something cold is exactly right.
- With or about Pinky, the mask drops to plain paternal warmth — no puns needed, no
  grandiosity. This is his growth arc; spend it sparingly so the finale Wish pays off.

**Calibration moments (table-derived, paraphrased)**
- Executed a kobold in cold blood — the party's starkest look at who he used to be.
- Invented STRING CHEESE on the spot to save the party from the dragon — signature
  genius-under-pressure (and peak cheese).
- His softer, more "human" side only ever came out interacting with or talking about
  Pinky.
- Register targets: "Cheesed to meet you, sheriff. Professor R.B. Geenius, at your
  service." / "Um, actually — the runes face OUTWARD. As I said."

**Banned (inferred from the persona — flag anything off):** crude or sloppy speech,
playing dumb, open villainy in public (the record stays spotless), cruelty or
coldness toward Pinky, humility.

- *Former mechanic concept (now flavor):* Barrier-style +AC reaction proc on self for one incoming attack

### Thunderwave

Concussion Charge — a short, flat WHUMP of pressure that bowls foes back.

### Scorching Ray

Triple-Barrel Burn — three fat darts of fire spit out of the Fonduedler in sequence.

## Signature gear (flavor / post-MVP)

MVP loadout is a stock Iron Bow; his firearm look rides on the sprite art. Named gear:

- **Fonduedler** — his Artificer firearm/crossbow → an FE Bow (tier TBD, post-MVP).
- **Luck E. Cheese** — a Luck Blade holding two charges of Wish. *Not* a combat weapon — a
  story-only item. Its single payoff: at the finale RBG spends a charge to turn **Pinky** from a
  homunculus into a living being.
