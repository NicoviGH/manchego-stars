### 🔑 The promotion seam (Ch 7 → 8)

- **Foreshadow in MVP:** the Ch 4 frost-druid boss drops a *flavored, locked relic*
  ("a crest of cold iron — it hums, but none of you know how to use it yet"). It
  sits in the convoy, unusable, as a Chekhov's gun for promotion.
- **Pay off at the seam:** the **first Master-Seal-equivalent** is obtained in/after
  the Revel's End break (Ch 8) — diegetically looted from the prison or earned in
  the escape. This matches FE8 holding promotions until the route-split era
  (`docs/fe8-pacing-reference.md §3`).
- **Promotions go live Ch 9–11** ([[manchego-stars-campaign-structure]]); PCs reach
  5e ~L11 / first FE promotion there. Specific crests (Knight Crest, Guiding Ring)
  may *flavor-appear* for an early single promotion, but the **Master Seal is the
  universal mechanism** (avoids class-matching headaches across 7 PCs).
